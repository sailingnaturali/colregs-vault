title: Vessels Constrained by Their Draft
verified: false

A vessel constrained by her draft may, in addition to the lights prescribed for power-driven vessels in Rule 23, exhibit where they can best be seen three allround red lights in a vertical line, or a cylinder. Vessel constrained by her draft—underway – 50 meters or greater in length.
